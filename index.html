About Me

My name is Stephen Moore. I am currently studing to become a full-stack web developer/software engineer. I have work in web development for more than 15 years but am now working to add to my skillset. I love technology and coding.

My other passion in life is judo. I have practied judo for just over 33 years. I am the head instructor along with my wife at Denver Judo. I am also a former World and Paralympic champion and have been the head coach of the USA Paralympic Judo Team since 20010.
